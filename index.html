What We Do

A private firm focused on proprietary research and systematic investing.

CAS Capitals deploys its own capital in Indian equity and derivatives markets, with a focus on research-led Futures & Options strategies.

The firm conducts internal research on market structures, macroeconomic trends, sector developments, and investment opportunities. Our process combines financial analysis with quantitative and data-driven methods.

CAS Capitals does not manage external capital and does not provide investment services to third parties.
