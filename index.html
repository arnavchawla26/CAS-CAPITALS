What We Do

A private firm focused on proprietary research and investing.

CAS Capitals deploys its own capital in Indian equity and derivatives markets, with a focus on research-led Futures & Options strategies.

The firm conducts internal research on market structures, macroeconomic trends, sector developments, and investment opportunities. Its research process combines financial analysis with quantitative and data-driven methods.

CAS Capitals does not manage external capital and does not provide investment services to third parties.

Proprietary Investing

Investment activity is conducted for the firm's own account using internal research and risk processes.
