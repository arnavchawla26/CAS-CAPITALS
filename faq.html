FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers to common questions about CAS Capitals — our research, technology, people, and how we operate.

What does CAS Capitals do?

CAS Capitals is a privately held investment and financial research firm. We deploy our own proprietary capital in Indian equity and derivatives markets using research-driven Futures & Options strategies. Our capabilities also span software development, AI & digital transformation, and infrastructure & construction.

Does CAS Capitals manage external capital or provide investment services?

No. CAS Capitals does not manage external capital and does not provide investment advisory or portfolio management services to third parties. All investment activity is conducted for the firm's own account.

What markets does the firm invest in?

The firm focuses on Indian financial markets, primarily equity and derivatives. Our research covers market structures, macroeconomic trends, sector developments, and investment opportunities within India.

What is the firm's research approach?

Our research process combines traditional financial analysis with quantitative and data-driven methods. We use internal research on market structures, macroeconomic context, sector dynamics, and alternative datasets to inform investment decisions for the firm's own capital.

Does the firm use AI and machine learning?

Yes. AI and machine learning are integral to our research and technology capabilities. We apply ML techniques to data analysis, pattern recognition, and building systems that support research and investment decisions.

What technology does CAS Capitals build?

We build proprietary software platforms, data pipelines, and analytics infrastructure that support our research and operations. This includes tools for market data analysis, risk management, and automation of research workflows.

How long has CAS Capitals been operating?

CAS Capitals was founded in 2015 and has over a decade of experience across investment research, software development, AI, and infrastructure.

Who leads the firm?

CAS Capitals is led by Arnav Chawla (Co-Founder & CEO), Archit (Co-Founder & Financial Analyst), and Vinod (Managing Director). The team combines financial expertise, quantitative research, and decades of market experience.

Does CAS Capitals offer jobs or internships?

Yes. We occasionally have openings for research analysts, software engineers, and other roles. Visit our Careers page to see current openings and apply.

How can I get in touch with CAS Capitals?

You can reach us through the Contact page. The firm also maintains professional profiles on LinkedIn and other platforms.

Still have questions?

Reach out directly — we're happy to help.
