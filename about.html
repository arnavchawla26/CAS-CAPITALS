About the Firm

A private investment and research firm focused on proprietary capital.

CAS Capitals is a privately held investment and financial research firm. The firm uses quantitative research, financial analysis, data analytics, and AI/ML to support its own proprietary investment activities in Indian financial markets. With over a decade of experience in software development, AI & digital transformation, and infrastructure, the firm brings a uniquely broad base of expertise to every engagement.

Our Mission

Research-led investing for Indian financial markets.

CAS Capitals deploys its own capital in Indian equity and derivatives markets, with a focus on research-led Futures & Options strategies.

The firm conducts internal research on market structures, macroeconomic trends, sector developments, and investment opportunities. Its research process combines financial analysis with quantitative and data-driven methods. Built on over a decade of experience, the firm's capabilities also span software development, AI & digital transformation, and infrastructure & construction.

CAS Capitals does not manage external capital and does not provide investment services to third parties.

Proprietary Investing

Investment activity is conducted for the firm's own account using internal research and risk processes.

Financial Research

Research spans market structures, macroeconomic context, sector analysis, and opportunity evaluation.

Data Analytics

Data science methods support research, risk management, and decision making across the firm.
